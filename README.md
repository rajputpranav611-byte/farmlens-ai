# 🌱 FarmLens AI

AI-powered crop disease detection platform built for **Bharat Academix CodeQuest 2026**.

## Problem Statement

Indian farmers often face crop losses due to delayed disease identification and lack of accessible agricultural guidance. Many farmers do not have immediate access to agricultural experts, resulting in reduced yield and increased treatment costs.

## Solution

FarmLens AI helps farmers identify crop diseases by analyzing images of crop leaves and providing:

* Disease detection
* Confidence score
* Severity assessment
* Treatment recommendations
* Scan history tracking
* Nearby agricultural store discovery
* Mobile-friendly experience

---

## Features

### 📷 Crop Disease Detection

Upload or capture a crop image and receive disease analysis.

### 📊 Disease Information

* Disease name
* Confidence percentage
* Severity level
* Suggested remedies

### 🕒 Scan History

Stores previous analyses for future reference.

### 🗺️ Nearby Agri Stores

Locate agricultural stores through an interactive map.

### 📱 Mobile-First Design

Optimized for smartphones and Progressive Web App deployment.
